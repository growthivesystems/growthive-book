FAQ

Questions, answered

Do my clients need to create an account to book?+

No. Clients enter their name, phone, and email to book — no account or app download required.

Can I control which appointments are confirmed?+

Yes. You choose automatic confirmation or manual approval for every booking on your page.

What happens if two people try to book the same time?+

Once a slot is booked, it disappears from the page immediately — so double bookings can't happen.

Can I cancel anytime?+

Yes, there's no lock-in contract. You can downgrade to the free Starter plan at any time.
